Beaches Around Baler

Explore the pristine shores and famous surf breaks of Aurora

Sabang Beach

The premier surfing hub with soft grey sands, active rollers, and vibrant accommodation boardwalks right by the sea.

Diguisit Beach

Famous for its dramatic, rugged rock formations breaking through crashing coastal tides, perfect for landscape photography.

Cemento Beach

Home to challenging break waves that attract advanced local and international surf experts seeking high-intensity tubes.
